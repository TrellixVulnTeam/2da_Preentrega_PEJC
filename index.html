Nirvana

Nirvana was an American grunge band from Aberdeen, Washington, United States founded by vocalist and guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic in 1987. Since its formation, Nirvana went through a series of 6 drummer changes (from the first to the last : Bob McFadden, Aaron Burckhard, Dale Crover, Dave Foster, Chad Channing and Dan Peters (who was only there to write the song (Sliver) until drummer Dave Grohl joined definitively in 1990. Nirvana is classified as a one of the most famous, important and influential bands in the history of music, being considered a symbol of his generation.
